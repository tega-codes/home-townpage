Top 3 activities to do in Lagos

Visit West Africa's Biggest Gallery

Discover a vast collection of contemporary African art and enjoy guided tours that showcase unique masterpieces from across the continent.

Explore Yoruba History and Culture At John Randle Centre

Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of the Yoruba people through interactive exhibits, cultural performances, and historical artifacts.

Sip and Cook With Friends

Join a fun cooking class to learn how to make local dishes, enjoy delicious food, and share memorable moments with friends while sipping wine.
